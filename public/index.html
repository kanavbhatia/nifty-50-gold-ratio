How this ratio works The Gold/Nifty ratio compares the price of 1g of gold (in ₹) to the Nifty 50 index level. Historically, when gold is cheap relative to Nifty, markets are overheating — shift into gold. When gold is expensive, markets are undervalued — shift into equities.
Ratio > 0.6 — Gold overvalued / Nifty cheap → reduce gold, buy stocks Ratio 0.28–0.6 — Both fairly valued → hold current allocation Ratio < 0.28 — Gold undervalued / Nifty expensive → increase gold, trim stocks
